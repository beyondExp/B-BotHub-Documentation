## Create your Expert

### Start to create your first expert

To create your first expert, click on the "Expert" Menu button on the left. Then click on the "Create New Expert" button.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="images/create-expert-second.png"
/>

### Fill in the details

Fill in the details of your expert. You can add a name, a profession and a description.
In the Background we will create all of the additional information for your expert for you.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="images/create-expert-third.png"
/>

### Add a profile picture

You can add a profile picture for your expert. This will be shown in the chat and in the expert overview.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="images/create-expert-fourth.png"
/>

### Add abilities

You can add abilities to your expert. These abilities are branches of behavior that your expert can have.
You can add as many abilities as you want. by pressing this button B-Bot will directly add a matching ability to your expert.
you can edit them afterwards. Be sure to not put long texts in here. You can change the behavior of your expert in the training tab.
these sentences are only for activating the behaviours and are resembling a "wake word" for the model to understand how it should act.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="images/create-expert-fifth.png"
/>

Above you can see an added ability. with the edit button you can change the ability and with the delete button you can delete the ability.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="images/create-expert-sixt.png"
/>

### Add templates

You can add templates to your expert. These templates are predefined questions the user can ask to the expert.
the controls are the same as for the abilities. You can add as many templates as you want.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="images/create-expert-seventh.png"
/>

### Choose your model

You can choose the model you want to use for your expert. You can choose between different models and different versions of the models.
also you can choose which provider you want to use for the model. the multipliers show how much the use of each model will cost you.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="images/create-expert-eight.png"
/>

### Save your expert

After you have filled in all the details you can save your expert. Your expert will be created and you can start to train or chat with it.

### Congratulations

You have created your first expert. Now you can train it and chat with it.
