## Overview

A virtual persona, also known as a digital or AI persona, is a sophisticated digital entity designed to interact with users in a human-like manner. These personas are powered by artificial intelligence and simulate human behavior and conversations, providing a dynamic and interactive user experience.

## Core Functionality

Virtual personas are designed to perform a variety of tasks, including:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="question" title="Answering Queries">
    Providing accurate and timely responses to user questions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="star" title="Personalized Recommendations">
    Suggesting tailored options based on user preferences.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="cart-shopping" title="Facilitating Transactions">
    Assisting in completing purchases or other transactions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
