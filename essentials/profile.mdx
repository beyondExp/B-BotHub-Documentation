## Edit your Profile

### Platform Settings

You can change your platform settings here. You can change your preferrences on getting notifications of any kind.

### Profile information

You can change your profile information here. You can change your name, your email your mobile number and your description.

### Logout

You can logout here. If you logout you will be redirected to the login page.

### App Connections

You can see all of your app connections here. You can see the name of the app and the type of the app. You can also see the status of the app connection.
